Introduction


Solar Powered Media explores energy responsive design. We experimented with ways of designing, building and hosting low energy websites with off-grid solar powered systems.


Server Setup


The website we developed ran from a single board computer(Raspberry Pi) powered by a solar panel setup.


Website Design


Solar Powered Media is energy responsive: the server's energy system inform the experience design and appearance of the site. The layout and the resolution of the assets change depending on the solar energy available. A higher resolution site is presented when the sun is shining, or when the battery is full, and the design of the site changes if less energy is available. A raw text version of the site is served when the battery is low, and it may go offline from time to time.
